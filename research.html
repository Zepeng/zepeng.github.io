Neutrino

My research focuses on understanding the fundamental properties of neutrinos.

Neutrinoless Double Beta Decay

I am involved in the search for neutrinoless double beta decay, a hypothetical nuclear process that, if observed, would prove that neutrinos are their own antiparticles (Majorana particles) and provide crucial information about the absolute neutrino mass scale.

Instrumentation R&D

My work includes the development and optimization of particle detection systems, with a particular focus on cryogenic detectors.

Machine Learning for Physics

I apply cutting-edge machine learning techniques to data analysis and deploying machine learning on edge computing for physics experiments.
